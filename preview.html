  Important information
 

 

Carry-On Baggage Allowance
Each passenger is only allowed to carry the corresponding Carry On Baggage for boarding as detailed below, with a total weight not exceeding 7 kg (15.4 lbs.). The Small Personal Item must not exceed the dimensions of 40 (L) x 25 (W) x 20 (H) cm and must fit under the seat in front of you. The Cabin Baggage (including wheels, handles and side pockets) must not exceed the dimensions of 56 (L) x 36 (W) x 23 (H) cm. Please note that all purchases made in the airports restricted area will be considered Carry On Baggage. Any excess items will incur additional fees and must be checked in.

Ultra Lite: 1 x Small Personal Item
Lite: 1 x Small Personal Item and 1 x Cabin Baggage
Essential: 1 x Small Personal Item.
Max: 1 x Small Personal Item and 1 x Cabin Baggage

For passengers who have booked tickets before 8 May 2024, please refer to your itinerary confirmation email from the initial booking for the designated Carry On Baggage Allowance or contact Customer Connect for more details.
